Community Groups

Campus Crusade for Christ is a community. It’s also a pretty big community, which makes it almost impossible to know everyone very well. Community groups are places to get to know a few people, really well. They are places to talk about real life issues and to go deeper into each others lives. They’re also places to ask questions, to wrestle with truths of the Bible and to grow in your faith.

Whether you are new to Cru, you’ve been hanging around waiting for an opportunity, or you’re not even “in” Cru, there is a Community Group on your part of campus that would love to have you join.
