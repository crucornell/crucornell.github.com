Upstate Fall Retreat

Mark your calendars for Upstate Fall Retreat, September 30th to October 2nd! We will be heading up to Seneca Lake to gather with students from all over the state for our fall retreat.

This will be a great opportunity to get out of town, hang out with friends, make some new friends, and connect with God.

The cost is only $20 if you have never been before, and it’s $55 if you have been (select “Paper Check” when you get to Payment Method to get the discount).

Check out http://www.upstatefallgetaway.com/ to register.
